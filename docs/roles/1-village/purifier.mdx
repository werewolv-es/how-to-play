## Purifier

The Purifier is a member of the Village who has the ability to exorcise
witchcraft from other players.

### Role Type

  - The Purifier is seen as member of the village by the Seer.
  - The Purifier is not seen as a user of witchcraft.
  - The Purifier is seen visiting by the Harlot/Stalker/Familiar.
  - The Purifier is not seen as a killer by the Adjudicator.

### Notes on the Purifier

  - Once per night you can attempt to purify a player, removing their
    ability to use Witchcraft.
  - Your visit will remove your targets ability if they are a witchcraft
    user
  - You will receive no notification whether your visit was successful
    or not
  - Evil witchcraft users include: All Coven Roles (including Puppets)
    and Shapeshifters
  - Good witchcraft users include: Seers, Protectors, Revivers,
    Beholders, Tarot Readers
  - Coven members will become Acolytes
  - Shapeshifters will become normal werewolves

### Tips for playing Purifier

  - If the Inquisition are in the game, they will be looking for you to
    help further their cause.
  - Purging Coven Members or Shapeshifters will hamper the available
    night actions of their teams.
  - You do not want to randomly use your ability as this can hinder the
    village ability to gather intel.

### Role Text

  - When a target is purged, they receive the a whisper from the
    Moderator: **"You feel dazed and confused. Not quite like you used
    to... You have the feeling that something bad happened to you during
    the night."**
